Science Communication Summer Institute (SciComm)

The Science Communication Summer Institute is the only program of its kind in the GTA. During its six weeks you’ll learn the theory and practical applications involved in sharing science knowledge across traditional and new media platforms. Offered in a non-traditional format, you’ll complete a 10-day on-campus residency from May 8-19 followed by online courses from May 22-June 16. Once you graduate you’ll be equipped with the knowledge and core abilities required of an effective science communicator. You’ll also have your own portfolio of work ready to be used in the field. If you’re a university or college science graduate (or near completion) who wants to become a better writer, storyteller and digitally engaged communicator, the Science Communication Summer Institute is the quickest way to achieve your objectives.
